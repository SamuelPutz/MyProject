Biography of Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi (1987) - footballer, was born on June 24 in the city of Rosario, Argentina. Best known as a striker for Barcelona.

In 2005, Messi became the star of Argentine football. This year in the biography of Lionel Messi brought many victories.
Thanks to his victory at the World Youth Championship, the athlete was named the best player in the competition. For the first time, Messi played for the national team on 17 August in a match against Hungary. In official matches, Lionel scored his first goal on May 1 for Barcelona. The opponents included another world famous football player - Ronaldinho. Then Messi was only 18. Soon Messi began to be compared to the Argentine football hero Diego Maradona. Like Maradona, Lionel was the team's scorer. The Barcelona official website calls Messi "supremely talented." In 2006, the athlete was part of the Argentine team at the World Championships.
Additional information:
The city of Rosario is also the birthplace of Che Guevara. On April 18, 2007, Messi scored an illustrious goal against Getafe. He kept the ball in midfield and then dashed past five defenders and a goalkeeper. This goal famous in the biography of Lionel Messi has been compared to Diego Maradona's “goal of the century” against England at the 1986 World Cup. Wayne Rooney is another teenage football sensation from the Messi era.

Lionel Messi. Achievements:

Team: Barcelona:

  • Champion of Spain (8): 2004/05, 2005/06, 2008/09, 2009/10, 2010/11, 2012/13, 2014/15, 2015/16
  • Spanish Cup Winner (5): 2008/09, 2011/12, 2014/15, 2015/16, 2016/17
  • Spanish Super Cup Winner (7): 2005, 2006, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2013, 2016
  • Champions League winner (4): 2005/06, 2008/09, 2010/11, 2014/15
  • UEFA Super Cup Winner (3): 2009, 2011, 2015
  • Club World Championship Winner (3): 2009, 2011, 2015

Argentina national team:

  • Olympic champion: 2008
  • World Championship silver medalist: 2014
  • America's Cup silver medalist (3): 2007, 2015, 2016
  • World Youth Championship Winner: 2005
